AI Financial Planner is a web-based financial intelligence system that helps users analyze, plan, and optimize their finances in real-time.

Unlike traditional budgeting tools, this application provides:

 Data-driven insights
 AI-based financial advice
 Goal & retirement planning
 Tax-saving recommendations
 Insurance gap detection

 PROBLEM STATEMENT
 user inputs age , income , expenses , existing investments and life goals. ai builds a complete , month by month financial roadmap: systematic investment plan(sip) amounts per goal, asset allocation shifts, insurance gaps , tax_saving moves, and emergency fund targets

SOLUTION:
AI Financial Planner transforms raw financial data into actionable intelligence using:

Financial algorithms
Rule-based AI logic
Predictive investment modeling

Features:

Smart Dashboard
Income vs Expenses vs Savings
Visual charts (Pie, Bar, Line)
Real-time financial summary

AI Smart Advice
Personalized suggestions like:
Increase SIP by 10%
Improve savings ratio 

Insurance Gap Detection
Calculates ideal life insurance coverage
Highlights underinsured users

Tax Optimization
Suggests deductions under:
Section 80C (ELSS, PPF)
Section 80D (Health Insurance)

Goal & Retirement Planning
Tracks financial goals
Displays deadlines
Calculates required monthly investments
